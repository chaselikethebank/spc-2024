Michelle Senters

The Unseen Companion

Topic: Parenting

+ Personal Bio +

Michelle Lynn Senters

Author of "The Unseen Companion"
(B.A. Education; M.Ed. Integrated Teaching Through the Arts) raised two daughters on her own and experienced many issues common to single mothers: financial crisis, emotional trauma in children, the pursuit of education, isolation from church, feelings of guilt/shame, consequences of fatherlessness, and the challenges of relationships and remarriage. Her message has been forged in her own journey as a single mom and has deepened through her years of mentoring single moms and launching ministries geared to them in the local church. She founded the Arise Ministry for Single Moms at her local church in Colorado Springs and this ministry is being considered by other area churches. She is a sought-after speaker at women's events, Bible studies, and writer's conferences.

Tasha Proske-Murphy
Head of Human Resources at
Newpark

Topic: Resumes and Interviews

+ Personal Bio +

Tasha Murphy

Tasha Murphy grew up in The Woodlands with a love for God, family, friends, travel, and the outdoors. In school, she gravitated towards marketing, though over her career, she found herself with a passion for the human side of the resources equation. Upon graduation from Texas A&M University Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Business Administration, International Business certificate, and a Master of Science in Marketing, she served clients across industries in the management consulting space before transitioning over to industry to work in HR for Anadarko Petroleum. While there, she climbed the ladder in various HR roles and leading large global teams, most notably leading HR for Mozambique LNG, a $20 billion-dollar mega-capital project. Prior to relocating her family to Colorado, her most recent role was serving as the Vice President of HR for Newpark Resources, reporting to the CEO with accountability to serve the Compensation Committee and Board of Directors on all things HR. As the global head of HR for Newpark, she and her team supported employees across 23 countries which, through the global pandemic, was no small feat! As amazing as the above experiences have been, Tasha’s calling and priority was always to be a mother. She is a mom of two daughters, a stepmom to two stepdaughters, and has walked the single-parent road. Blissfully happy to be married to her wonderful husband, Michael (who has also walked this road!), she knows full well the blessings and challenges that come with life after divorce. She and her family live in Steamboat Springs with a rambunctious Bernese Mountain Dog puppy, shuttling children to and from activities, snow skiing or hiking on the weekends, and running a local business that she and Michael purchased to “dream line” their future together in their happy place!

Julie Crocker
M.Ed., M.Ed., B.Ed.
Counselor at Magnolia Creek Counseling

Topic: Parenting with Love & Logic

+ Personal Bio +

Julie Crocker

Julie Crocker is a Licensed Professional Counselor Associate in the State of Texas. She has worked in education and ministry for 25+ years. As her growing community emerged from Covid, she saw the alarming need for practicing Christian counselors. Julie decided to leave her position in children's ministry to open her counseling practice, Magnolia Creek Counseling where she serves children, teens and families. In addition to a bachelors degree in education from Samford University, she also holds a masters degree in college administration from the University of Arkansas, and a masters degree in counseling from Liberty University. She is passionate about the topic of fear, worry, and anxiety and our kids! She is supervised by Jennifer J Cain, LPC-S, RPT-S.

Breakouts

Doug Garrison
CFP at Cerity Partners

Topic: Financial Peace University

+ Personal Bio +

Doug Garrison

Doug Garrison is a native Texan who grew up in southeastern Illinois. He graduated from Yale University and received his MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania. Following graduate school, he joined Exxon, now ExxonMobil. His 31-year career with ExxonMobil included Human Resources (HR) assignments in several different locations, including assignments in Brazil, New Jersey, and Texas. Following retirement from ExxonMobil, Doug studied for and passed a qualifying exam required to obtain his Certified Financial Planner™ certification. His “encore career” has now lasted over 13 years and he is currently a partner in the Houston office of Cerity Partners, a nationally-recognized independent wealth management firm. Doug and his wife, Geni, have three daughters and have been members of The Woodlands Methodist Church since 2003.

Breakouts

Allison Stephan
MA, LPC-Associate
Therapist, Athlete, Coach

Topic: Being Content in Singleness and Dating

+ Personal Bio +

Allison Stephan

Allison Stephan is a Licensed Professional Counselor practicing in The Woodlands. She spent 20 years as a journalist before completing the master’s programs in both marriage and family therapy and professional counseling. Allison believes that stories weave the fabric of who we are as people—that they have transformative power to allow us to see ourselves and others through a different lens. They link us to our past and inspire hope for our futures. The ability to re-author a story allows the discovery of new possibilities, contentment, and healing. Allison’s specialties include couples, families, adoption and neurodivergent populations.
Allison is the mom of two kids, two dogs, two turtles and a bearded dragon. She enjoys books, music, art, and a healthy dose of exercise in her free time. She has completed more than a few Ironman races and has a deep love for human performance and enjoys helping athletes achieve their best in not only their physical performance, but in their mental game as well. She is a personal trainer, Pilates instructor and Spinning instructor and enjoys seeing people gain perspective through movement.

Melissa Williams
Comedian

2024 Emcee

+ Personal Bio +

Melissa Williams

While living in Arizona, her parents got married 13 days before she was born. After their divorce, Melissa moved to Texas with her mom and two siblings. In high school, she broke the record for the longest false start to date (or ever) in addition to failing her floral design class in the same year. She has never been married, but she delivered an 11-pound, 4-ounce baby. (If you’re wondering, they both survived.) Her daughter is now an amazing 27-year-old woman. Melissa is still in awe that she managed to keep another human alive this long. She loves time with her family, who live in the same town as her. (It is important to note that her Thanksgiving turkey has been voted “the best” in her family three years in a row.) Growing up with a stutter, Melissa spent years in speech therapy and never dreamed she would be speaking in front of hundreds of people. By the grace of God, she has used theater and the creative arts to overcome her speech impediment and is now invited to stages throughout the U.S. to make people laugh. Her speech therapist would be so proud- and maybe a little shocked! She will make you laugh. She will make your friends laugh, and she may even make that one person in your life that hasn't smiled in 17 months or even years laugh! She has been a keynote speaker at a wide variety of events. Whether it be a women's retreat, church or corporate function, party, or wedding, she has brought clean comedy to captive audiences through sharing relatable stories of everyday life. She brings laughter that serves as medicine. She laughs out loud and thinks everyone else should too. (Like that one time she was asked to leave 59 Diner because her laugh was too loud. Yes. That happened!)
Life is better when laughing together.

Bristol House
Music Collective
Altars

2024 Musical Guest

+ Group Bio +

Bristol House

Bristol House is a worship movement that exists to steward the heritage of the Wesleyan people while pursuing a great awakening.
"Altars" was created out of faithful waiting. These songs are a piece of us and each of our journeys with the Lord. We invite you to go on the journey of finding altar moments with us. Always remember that in faithful waiting, meet at the altar. In fervent prayer, meet at the altar. When singing joyful noise, meet at the altar. In peace and discomfort, meet at the altar.
